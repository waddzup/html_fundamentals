Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

For one person but just double the eggs and milk for two

  • One medium or large egg
  • Milk - same volume as the egg
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • knob of butter

Instructions

  1. Break the egg into a bowl
  2. Add milk to double the volume
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste
  4. Mix the egg and milk using a fork or whisk until combined
  5. Heat the butter in a frying pan on medium heat
  6. When the butter is bubbling add the milk and egg mixture
  7. Stir continually with a spatular until the mixture sets
  8. Remove from the heat
  9. Serve on buttered toast and add grated cheese if you like this
